About Matt Aimonetti

Bio

Matt Aimonetti is Splice cofounder, an experienced technologist, entrepreneur, technical writer and active open-source contributor. Based in Southern California. Prior to Splice, Matt worked as a Tech manager/developer at LivingSocial, on video game development at Sony PlayStation and on many other things.
